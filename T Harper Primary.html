Overview

The intent of the project was for the student to demonstrate the integration of several technologies that have explored over the span of several months. We had the opportunity to use a provided data set, or create our own. I opted to create my own data set. Let's take a deeper dive into the data set, and get a better understanding on the data presentation that is available. I opted to gather my own data, and pulled data through an API from Open Weather using a criteria of latitude and longitude coordinates. The intent was to determine whether or not latitude had any impact on humidity, cloudiness, wind speed and temperature in cities around the world. We used Matplotlib to preset the Scatterplots of the values. The following charts is a considation of data from over 500 cities across the globe.
